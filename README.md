# whatsapp_chat_analysis

This repo contains whatsapp analysis of a group (of 65 members) from 4 december,2018 to 3 july,2020. Click on Analysis_Results to see:
   Busiest day,
   Busiest day of week,
   Busiest hours,
   Top 6 active members,
   Top members who shared media,
   Top messages grouped by month and year,
   Wordcloud for top 100 words
